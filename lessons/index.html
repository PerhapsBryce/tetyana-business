Get Started

I specialize in teaching students of all ages, starting from the age of 5 and including adults. With my accelerated teaching method and approach, students are able to learn at a faster pace and retain information more effectively. According to the student's age and goal, I audition the students to determine where they will be placed: in 30 minute, 45 minute, 60 minute, or 90 minute sessions.
