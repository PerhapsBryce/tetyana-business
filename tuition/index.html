Get Started

Do you want to learn how to play the piano?


Tetyana Guthrie offers a comprehensive piano program that caters to students of all levels. With over 20 years of experience teaching, she has the expertise to guide students from the very beginning to a professional level. Through the 12-week sessions, students will not only learn essential piano skills such as sight-reading and chord recognition but also receive a thorough education in music theory and history. The program runs year-round and includes 12-week sessions that culminate in a mandatory recital where students are expected to perform at least four memorized pieces. Classes are tailored to the student, with 30 minute intervals, 45 minute intervals, 60 minute intervals, or 90 minute intervals. The payment of each student is expected on the 1st of each month for the total four sessions in advance to ensure the student's time is reserved. On request by the student or in the event of inclement weather, the sessions can be conducted online via Zoom. Should the student need to cancel a class, they should inform me no less than 24 hours in advance in order to be able to make up the class. Whether you are just starting out or looking to refine your skills, Tetyana Guthrie's piano program is designed to help you reach your full potential!
