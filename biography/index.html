Throughout her career, Tetyana has had the privilege of learning from a diverse group of accomplished pianists. One of her most notable mentors is Dr. Mykola Suk, a renowned Ukrainian-American pianist who currently teaches at UNLV, where Tetyana is pursuing her PhD in piano performance under his guidance. She also had the opportunity to serve as a graduate teaching assistant at Denver University's Lamont School of Music, where she was mentored by Stephanie Cheng and gained valuable experience teaching students of varying skill levels. These experiences have enriched her musical education and have helped shape her into a skilled and knowledgeable pianist and educator.
