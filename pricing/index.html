Get Started

Do you want to learn how to play the piano?


Tetyana Guthrie offers a comprehensive piano program that caters to students of all levels. With over 20 years of experience teaching, she has the expertise to guide students from the very beginning to a professional level. Through the 12-week sessions, students will not only learn essential piano skills such as sight-reading and chord recognition but also receive a thorough education in music theory and history. The program runs year-round and includes 12-week sessions that culminate in a mandatory recital where students are expected to perform at least four memorized pieces. Classes are tailored to beginners with 60-minute intervals($60) and intermediate to advanced students with 90-120 ($90-$120) minute intervals. Whether you are just starting out or looking to refine your skills, Tetyana Guthrie's piano program is designed to help you reach your full potential
